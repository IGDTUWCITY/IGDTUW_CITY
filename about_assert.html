Primary goal

To simplify the journey into blockchain and Web3 for freshers by providing a supportive and engaging environment, fostering peer learning, and creating opportunities for skill-building and networking. Our aim is to familiarize members with evolving technologies and explore the future of financial growth in this sector under the guidance of experienced seniors and supportive peers.

Description

AssetMerkle is more than just a society; it's a family where students can explore blockchain and Web3 technologies. We are dedicated to creating a fun and safe space for learning and growth. Our society hosts major events, including 24-hour hackathons and workshops at the Microsoft Office, and connects with top institutions like DTU, NSUT, and IIITs. Freshers receive all the support they need in a zero-pressure environment, perfect for exploration. By joining AssetMerkle, you’ll find friends, mentors, and a second family to share your journey with. Our community is buzzing with activities, from exciting events to relaxed, engaging learning sessions. We pride ourselves on being the most active club on campus, making waves in the wider Web3 space and bringing together over 300 participants from all over India for our hackathons. At AssetMerkle, you'll get insider knowledge from experienced seniors, make lifelong friends, and enjoy a chill and fun learning environment. Join us to help organize cool events, shape the future of blockchain together, and be part of something bigger and trendier than you can imagine. Let’s make this journey unforgettable!
