Nirvana Orientation Itinerary

Date: September 4th, 2024
Time: 8:00 PM
Platform: Google Meet

* Introduction and Icebreaker Quiz
We’ll kick off the session with a fun, random Icebreaker Quiz to get everyone warmed up! This quiz is designed to be light-hearted and interactive, giving juniors a chance to bond with seniors. The top 3 participants will receive special Instagram Shoutouts from our official page!

* Briefing about Nirvana
After the quiz, we’ll dive into what Nirvana is all about—our goals, our past projects, and how we operate. We’ll introduce the Nirvana team and the seniors who have been leading various initiatives, giving you insight into how we work as a community of creators.

* Upcoming Competition Announcement
Get ready for some excitement! We’ll be announcing details about our upcoming design competition. As you may have seen on our Instagram page, Nirvana is known for its amazing competitions where we give out medals and cool gifts to winners. Check out our past competitions and see how we celebrate creativity!

* End-to-End Project Mentorship
Selected juniors will get the opportunity to work on a project under the mentorship of seniors. We’ll help you create an end-to-end project for your portfolio or resume—a crucial addition that will be super valuable in the engineering and tech world!

* Surprise Design Game
Next up, we’ll be hosting a surprise design game that will put your creativity to the test! It’ll be a fun and interactive session where you can showcase your design thinking and problemsolving skills.

* Q&A Session
We’ll wrap things up with a Q&A session where you can ask anything about Nirvana, the projects, competitions, or even tips on how to improve your design skills.

We’re excited to see you there! Prepare for a fun-filled and informative evening with your fellow Nirvana members.
