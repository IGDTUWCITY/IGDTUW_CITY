🌷 Warm Rotaract Greetings! 🌷

Hair loss during cancer treatment can profoundly impact a patient’s self-esteem and spirit. But you have the power to make a difference!

The Rotaract Club of IGDTUW, in partnership with Aarogya Seva and Khoobsurat Salon, is thrilled to invite you to 🌟 Strands of Strength: A Hair Donation Drive for Cancer Patients 🌟

This is a beautiful opportunity for us to come together and make a tangible difference in the lives of those battling cancer. By donating your hair, you can help restore confidence and bring hope to those who need it most by providing free wigs to cancer patients.

💇‍♀️ Still Unsure ?
