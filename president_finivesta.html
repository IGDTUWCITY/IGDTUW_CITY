Meet the President

Khushi Sharma

Khushi Sharma, the President of Finivesta, is dedicated to enhancing financial literacy among women. Under her leadership, Finivesta emphasizes the importance of financial education as a means for women to achieve their full potential and contribute to a more equitable society. The organization offers a variety of learning opportunities, including workshops, seminars, and other events focused on finance and economics. Additionally, Finivesta provides mentorship and networking opportunities, connecting women with professionals in the financial sector. Through these initiatives, Khushi Sharma and Finivesta strive to empower women to make informed financial decisions and advance their financial independence.

Tejasvi Singh

Tejasvi, the other President of Finivesta, works alongside Khushi Sharma to promote financial literacy among women. She shares the vision of empowering women through financial education, enabling them to make informed financial decisions and achieve their full potential.
